üî¨What Hematologist Researchers Do

Hematologist researchers are doctors who specialize in blood disorders. We study diseases like leukemia, anemia, and blood clots, then work to find better treatments. It's part doctor, part scientist - treating patients while also doing research to help future patients.

üìöThe Path to Get There

College (4 years)
Pre-med with biology, chemistry, physics, and math
Medical School (4 years)
Earn MD or DO degree
Residency (3 years)
Internal medicine training
Fellowship (3 years)
Specialize in blood disorders
